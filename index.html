About

Over time I have taken pictures on my camera to remember the differnet scotch whiskys I have tried, but my memory doesn't always remember the taste or if I liked it or didn't. I've created this site so that I could input enough information into it to remember. To remember what it was like to taste the whisky. To smell the whisky. To know if I should try it again or pass it by.
